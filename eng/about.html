Hi, I'm Angelo

Welcome to my portfolio. If you want to explore my recent work, projects, and user flows, you can find them here: My Work

Uncovering our limits, identifying errors, and knowing how to acknowledge them — as well as recognizing our own biases — is what drives us to continuously iterate, improve our designs, and truly help users. This is what makes us great professionals, and it is a value that goes far beyond pure aesthetics.
Evolution

I started my professional journey with a solid background as a Front-end Developer, an experience that shaped my first steps in the design world through the technical construction of fully responsive layouts, web interfaces, and applications. This taught me from day one to think in terms of structure, technical constraints, and interface feasibility.

At a certain point in my career, however, I felt a deep need: I wanted to go beyond *how* an element is built; I wanted to understand *why*. This led me to pivot and specialize vertically in UX. This transition completely changed not only my way of designing, but above all, my way of thinking.

Today, this evolution has led me to be more than just a designer who draws screens—I am someone who builds real products, always putting the user first. Thanks to this experience, I can handle the entire lifecycle of a project: from initial user research and interviews to delivering a logical framework fully prepared to be translated into code.

Areas of Expertise

An integrated approach combining user research, technical feasibility, and seamless interaction.

UX/UI Design

Through user research, I dive deep into understanding the people who use a product. This allows me to uncover their background, motivations, pain points, emotions, and goals, translating them into clear and functional interfaces.

Front-End Development

I translate visual product design into production-ready code. I do this by building web and application interfaces using components that are scalable, reusable, natively responsive, and fully accessible to everyone.

UI Motion

Often overlooked, for me Motion Design is an essential part of UX. I use it to enhance interaction and convey emotion, but always while asking a fundamental question: am I truly improving the user experience?
